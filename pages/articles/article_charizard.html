Charizard

Charizard is a Fire and Flying-type Pokémon and belongs to the rare third evolution, beginning with Charmander. Charizard is renowned for its impressive appearance, with fiery wings and a flaming tail. It is an iconic representative of Fire-type Pokémon and one of the symbols of the franchise.

This powerful Pokémon possesses fire-based attacks such as "Flamethrower" and "Fly." Being a Flying-type, Charizard can not only attack from the air but also carry its trainer on its back, making it a reliable and swift mode of transportation in the Pokémon world.
